I am a second-year undergraduate student majoring in biology with a minor in general business, while on a pre-dental track at the University of California, Riverside.

I have experience working in a hospital setting as a paid summer intern at two Sutter Health hospitals in San Francisco. Additionally, I worked as a garden intern for my church's community garden during the summer going into my second year of college.

I plan to attend dental school and obtain a DDS (Doctor of Dental Surgery) degree with the hope of becoming an oral and maxillofacial surgeon practicing in the San Francisco Bay Area. I am also interested in making oral hygiene more accessible for disadvantaged communities, by creating my own line of oral hygiene products that will consist of tablet mouthwash that can be activated with water and toothpaste that remineralizes teeth. I am a firm believer that everyone should have access to proper dental care and oral hygiene education. Most people think that it is important to brush their teeth to only prevent cavities and plaque build up, but it goes much deeper than that. Poor oral hygiene is linked to an increased risk for heart disease, cancer, diabetes, and gum disease. I will make it a priority to provide appropriate oral hygiene education and care to my future patients as well as help them take preventative measures to ensure healthy teeth and instill confidence in them to show off their beautiful smile to others.
